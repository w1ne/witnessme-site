Why this blocker

  • The tax is physical
    A breathing screen costs you nothing. Ten pushups costs you something. Friction that actually changes behavior.
  • No subscription
    Free. Tip jar later if you want to support development. No paywall, no upgrade pop-ups, no "Pro" gates.
  • Anonymous leaderboard
    Climb from DUST to VALHALLA on weekly rep totals. No name, no email — just a handle like IRON_WITNESS_42. Duolingo leagues, brutalist coat of paint.
  • Brutalist design
    Black, white, red, monospace. Looks like a propaganda poster, not a wellness app. The aesthetic is the moat.
  • Optional share
    #witnessme on TikTok, IG, YouTube. The hashtag is the feed.

What we don't collect

  • Your name, email, phone, address, or any other personal identifier
  • Camera footage — pose detection is fully on-device, frames never leave your phone
  • Contacts, calendar, location, health data
  • Which apps you tried to open, when, or how often
  • Information from inside the apps you block
